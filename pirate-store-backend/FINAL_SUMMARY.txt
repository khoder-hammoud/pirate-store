📋 REFUND FLOW | دورة معالجة الاسترجاع
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  👤 User creates refund
         ↓
  💾 Save to database (status: pending)
         ↓
  ⚙️ Process automatically (setImmediate)
         ↓
  🔄 Retry up to 3 times
         ↓
  ✅ Success or ❌ Failed
         ↓
  🔗 Update Order automatically
         ↓
  ✨ Done!
